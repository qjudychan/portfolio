  HABIT  

(WORK IN PROGRESS)
One's time-inconsistent preferences cause misalignment between their intentions and ultimate actions and are a common pitfall in trying to develop good habits

The Habit app endeavors to help those in pursuit of self improvement help themselves by bringing behavioral research insights to productivity tools (to-do lists, timers, counters)
